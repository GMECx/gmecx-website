What we do

Four services, one discipline

From design-phase reviews to post-occupancy monitoring, GMEC covers the full life of a building enclosure.

Building Enclosure Commissioning (BECx)

A full suite of BECx services that delivers efficient, durable, code-compliant envelopes — from OPR development through post-occupancy lessons-learned. Design review, submittal review, field observations, compliance verification, and final airtightness testing.

Learn more →

Building Envelope Assessments

Whole-building multi-point airtightness testing, guided leakage diagnostics, and clear written reports for existing buildings of any size — from tiny houses to commercial and institutional. Add-ons: thermal imaging, theatrical fogging, window water penetration testing.

Learn more →

Remote Monitoring Programs

Customized long-term monitoring using WiFi- or cellular-enabled battery-powered sensors embedded in building assemblies. Track temperature, humidity, wood moisture, CO, CO₂, building air pressure, exterior weather, and A/C energy use — for up to 15 years.

Learn more →

BEAM — Embodied Carbon Modeling

Upfront embodied-carbon accounting for building materials, using BEAM's dimensional models and global EPD database. Category-wide and component-level comparisons that inform emissions-reduction decisions during design.

Learn more →
